# Personas

## Owner persona: NRI landowner

- Lives outside India for most of the year
- Owns one or more vacant plots in a hometown or growth corridor
- Is worried about encroachment, illegal construction, and long response times
- Values proof, speed, and reliability more than feature novelty
- Often coordinates with family members across time zones

## Delegate persona: Family representative

- Based locally or semi-locally
- May inspect or coordinate when something happens
- Needs controlled access, not full administrative authority
- Wants simple incident summaries and clear next actions

## Ops admin persona

- Monitors incoming alerts and verifies incident context
- Needs a clean queue, strong status semantics, and operational logs
- Responsible for escalation and response quality

## Partner persona: Local response team

- Receives dispatches for site visits
- Needs concise job context, site location, and proof expectations
- Must upload structured closure notes and evidence

## Installer persona

- Surveys the plot and positions devices for coverage
- Needs field documentation on placement, power assumptions, and maintenance checks
- Feeds install metadata back into the system
