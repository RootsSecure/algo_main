# Product Vision

## Vision statement

Enable NRIs to feel continuously informed and operationally supported when they own vacant land plots in India, even when they live thousands of kilometers away. The product should reduce anxiety, improve time-to-awareness, and create a trustworthy evidence trail whenever suspicious activity occurs.

## Why this matters

Vacant land plots are uniquely vulnerable because there is often no occupied structure, no trusted daily observer, and no reliable record of what happened between visits. Owners typically discover problems late, when illegal occupation, unauthorized construction, broken gates, or tampered boundaries are already expensive to reverse.

## Product promise

NRI Plot Sentinel combines plot-specific monitoring, real-time alerts, and verified response coordination so owners can:

- Know when something unusual happens
- Distinguish signal from camera noise
- See site-health issues before coverage silently disappears
- Escalate verified incidents with evidence in hand

## Experience principles

- Clarity over noise: alerts must be understandable and prioritized
- Verification before panic: the system should assist with validation, not flood owners
- Evidence over ambiguity: every incident should leave a usable record
- Operational empathy: the product should assume owners are remote, busy, and working across time zones
- Plot-first design: workflows should match vacant land realities, not occupied smart-home assumptions

## North-star outcomes

- Owners trust the platform enough to depend on it during long absences
- Critical incidents move from detection to human action within minutes
- Device outages are visible before owners lose effective site coverage
- Every resolved incident improves future site setup and escalation rules
