# Owner Onboarding Guide

## Step 1: Create or use an owner account
Use the owner registration or login endpoints.

## Step 2: Add the plot
Provide the name, address, city, state, geo-boundary, site type, risk profile, and escalation plan.

## Step 3: Add zones
Create zones for gates, vulnerable edges, or sensitive access points.

## Step 4: Register devices
Link devices to the property with clear install locations and vendor details.

## Step 5: Validate site health
Send a heartbeat or confirm heartbeat ingestion from the deployed device.

## Step 6: Prepare local support
Invite a delegate if the owner wants a family member to view the property, and register a response partner if field visits are needed.
