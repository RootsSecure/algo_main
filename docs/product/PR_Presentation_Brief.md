## 1. Executive Summary
**RootsSecure** is a premium, edge-first security platform designed for Non-Resident Indians (NRIs) who own vacant land plots in India. Bridging the gap between continents, it replaces traditional, unreliable property inspections with a high-fidelity "Digital Panopticon"—a system that is always watching, always thinking, and ready to act.

---

## 2. The Narrative: Why RootsSecure?
### The "Vacant Plot" Crisis
For an NRI, a vacant plot in India isn't just an asset; it's a source of constant anxiety. Unlike occupied homes, vacant land has:
- **No Eyes**: No daily residents to spot trouble.
- **No Memory**: No record of when encroachment or illegal construction started.
- **No Speed**: Owners often discover issues months late, when legal reversals are nearly impossible.

### The RootsSecure Promise
We give owners **operational support** thousands of miles away. We don't just provide "cameras"; we provide a **trusted evidence trail** and a **verified response workflow**.
